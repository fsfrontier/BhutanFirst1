About Us

Located in the capital city of Bhutan, Thimphu, Bhutan Deki Yoezer Tours & Treks is a licensed tour operator in Bhutan. Under the guidance and support of a Professional Tour Operator, experienced employees manage it. Registered with the erstwhile Tourism Council of Bhutan and licensed by the Department of Trade under the Ministry of Economic Affairs, it is a trustworthy travel service provider in Bhutan.

We have bountiful experiences in managing various types of tours and treks in Bhutan. We stand by you and your group for the outstanding experiences of your visit to Bhutan. Whether FIT or Group, it is our priority to provide services beyond expectation. We are a group of enthusiastic travel service providers, and our itineraries are customizable at any time. If you want different vibes, we always welcome you to contact us anytime. We are here to make your visit to Bhutan, the most memorable one.

Well-experienced travel guides will share episodes on Bhutan one after another. Our drivers will drive around through the meandering road where you will see beautiful landscapes. Our guides will share with you the social, cultural, religious, and natural, flora and fauna, and so on. As a team, we provide tour packages like Culture, Trekking, Nature, Weddings in Bhutanese style, Honeymoon, Anniversary, Photography, Study, Festival, Mountain Biking, MICE, and Buddhist Pilgrimage tours in Bhutan.

Why Us?

As an established tour operator, we have an experienced team, outstanding customizable itineraries, a focus on sustainability, and a greater future. We are committed to delivering tailored services per clients’ needs.

For those who haven’t heard about Bhutan, we are here to provide you with detailed and authentic information before planning the trip. The prices are transparent and accountable as well for affordability.
